## Instructions

### Instant Pot Method
1. **Soak chickpeas** from **Prep** for at least 4+ hours or overnight, then drain
2. Press **SAUTE** on Instant Pot. Heat oil, add cumin seeds and bay leaf. Let cumin splutter
3. Add ingredients from **Saute** section: onions, green chilies, ginger-garlic paste. **Saute 3-4 minutes** until onions turn light brown
4. Add tomato puree and all spices from **Cook** section (red chili powder, turmeric, chole masala, coriander powder). **Cook 2 minutes**
5. Add soaked chickpeas, water, and salt from **Cook** section. **Stir well**
6. Close lid, set pressure valve to **SEALING**. Select **PRESSURE COOK/MANUAL** (High Pressure) for **30 minutes**
7. Do **NATURAL PRESSURE RELEASE** (NPR) when done
8. Add **Finishing** ingredients: amchur powder, kasuri methi, coriander leaves
9. If too watery, turn on **SAUTE** mode and simmer 5 minutes to thicken
10. Serve hot with bathura, pooris, chapatis, or rice

### Notes
- Chickpeas should be soft and easily mashed when pressed
- Mash a few chickpeas with the back of a ladle for thicker consistency
- Curry will thicken as it cools
