## Instructions

1. Heat oil in large saucepan over medium heat
2. Add **base** vegetables (onions, carrots, celery, garlic)
3. Cook gently for 15-20 mins until softened
4. Stir in all **sauce** ingredients (lentils, tomatoes, purée, herbs, stock)
5. Bring to a simmer
6. Cook for 40-50 mins until lentils are tender and sauce is thick
7. Add water if needed during cooking
8. Season to taste
9. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to packet instructions
10. Drain pasta and divide between bowls
11. Spoon sauce over pasta and top with grated parmesan

## Notes

- Freezes well for up to 3 months
- Can be refrigerated for up to 3 days
- Defrost overnight at room temperature before reheating
- Makes excellent lasagne sauce
- Recipe from Good Food magazine, March 2011
