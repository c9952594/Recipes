## Ingredients

### Rice

Ingredient | Amount | Calories | Preparation
--- | --- | --- | ---
Sushi Rice | 3 cups |  | 
Water | 3.5 cups |  | 
Rice Vinegar | 0.5 cup |  | 
White Sugar | 2 tbsp |  | 
Fine Salt | 2 tsp |  | 

## Instructions

1. Rinse sushi rice until water runs mostly clear.
2. Cook rice with water.
3. Heat rice vinegar, sugar, and salt until dissolved.
4. Fold seasoning into cooked rice while warm.
5. Cool to room temperature before using.
