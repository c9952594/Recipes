## Ingredients

### Sauce

Ingredient | Amount | Calories | Preparation
--- | --- | --- | ---
Flour | 70g |  | 
Butter | 70g |  | 
Milk | 700g |  | Warmed
Extra Mature Cheddar | 300g |  | Grated
Garlic | 10g |  | Crushed

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
2. Add flour and stir to form a roux.
3. Gradually add warm milk while stirring until smooth.
4. Add cheddar and garlic and stir until melted and combined.
5. Bring just to a gentle boil, then remove from heat.
