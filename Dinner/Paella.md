## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a large lidded frying pan over medium heat. Fry onion for 5 minutes.
2. Add peppers and cook 5 more minutes until softened.
3. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute, then add smoked paprika and cook for 1 minute.
4. Stir in paella rice and cook for 2 minutes.
5. Pour in white wine (if using) and reduce for 2 to 3 minutes.
6. Add chopped tomatoes, vegetable stock, and saffron (if using).
7. Stir in beans, bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer for 10 minutes.
8. Scatter green beans or broccoli over the top, cover, and cook for 3 to 4 minutes until rice and greens are tender.
9. If needed, cook uncovered for a few extra minutes to reduce liquid.
10. Remove from heat, squeeze over lemon juice, and serve with lemon wedges.
