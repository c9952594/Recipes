## Instructions

1. Season sweet potato and peppers, then roast at 220C for 20 minutes.
2. Fry sweetcorn until lightly browned.
3. Add chipotle paste and garlic and fry for 1 minute.
4. Add remaining **Soup** ingredients and boil for 5 minutes.
5. Add roasted sweet potato and peppers.
6. Season to taste, then serve with **Topping**.
