## Instructions

1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat and cook the shallot for 3-4 minutes until softened.
2. Add garlic, salt, white pepper, Italian herbs, and chipotle flakes; cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
3. Add tomatoes, tomato concentrate, balsamic vinegar, and bouillon. Stir well and bring to a gentle simmer.
4. Simmer uncovered for 20-25 minutes until the tomatoes have broken down.
5. Remove from the heat, add cottage cheese and basil, and blend until smooth.
6. Taste and adjust seasoning, then serve hot.
