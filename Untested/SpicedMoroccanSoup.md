## Instructions

1. Halve, peel and thinly slice the red onion. Drain and rinse the chickpeas. Roughly chop the coriander.
2. Heat 15ml oil in a large saucepan over medium-low heat. Add the onion and cook until soft, 4-5 mins.
3. Add the ground cumin, ras-el-hanout and smoked paprika. Cook for 1 min, then season with salt.
4. Add the chopped tomatoes, tomato puree, water and vegetable stock pot. Stir to dissolve and bring to the boil.
5. Add the red split lentils and chickpeas. Simmer for 20-25 mins until the lentils are soft.
6. Meanwhile, preheat the grill to high. Place the ciabatta halves cut-side up on a baking tray, spoon over 15ml olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and grill for 3-4 mins until golden.
7. Season the soup to taste with salt and pepper.
8. Serve in bowls topped with coriander and a spoonful of Greek yoghurt, with the toasted ciabatta on the side.
