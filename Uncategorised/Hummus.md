## Ingredients

### Blend

Ingredient | Amount | Calories | Preparation
--- | --- | --- | ---

Cumin | 0.5 tsp |  | 
Olive Oil | 5 tbsp |  |

### Finish

Ingredient | Amount | Calories | Preparation
--- | --- | --- | ---
Paprika | To garnish |  | 

## Instructions

1. Blend chickpeas, garlic, tahini, lemon juice, and cumin.
2. Add olive oil and blend again.
3. Add salt and pepper to taste.
4. Transfer to a bowl and garnish with paprika.
